Terminal Legend

"Base Camp: The Escape" is a text-based horror adventure game where players must uncover the dark secrets of their software bootcamp and escape. Their mentors, Sean, Brittany, and RJ, are not who they seem and plan to sacrifice students to a "software god" named Kagan. To escape, players must navigate the campus, find the secret code, and make it out alive. The game features a retro terminal aesthetic, a narrated experience, an interactive story, mini-games, and five possible endings. Players must navigate the campus, find the code, and face off against Ms. Brittany in a boss fight. The game offers an immersive horror atmosphere with text-to-speech narration and eerie background music.
